A short Introduction

Hello and welcome! My name is Aditya. I am a Ph.D. Scholar at the Department of Physics, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur from July, 2022. I am working with Dr. Jyotirmoy Bhattacharya in theoretical high-energy physics. My research involves bootstrapping certain quantum mechanical systems at finite temperature. Parallely, I am also studying dualities between certain Chern-Simons matter theories in curved backgrounds.

I did my BS and MS in Physics from Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Tirupati.

In my spare time, I choose a sample from a set of my hobbies. The set includes: reading novels, cycling and writing.
